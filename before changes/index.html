About us


Who are we?






We are a platform.

A platform that calls together the members of the community to promote peace, infuse integrity, boost goodwill and work towards creating a better and sustainable world.




We are a book.

The blank pages of which, we hope to fill with a cluster of inventive solutions, achievements, and triumphs against the impediments to the development of the community on all fronts.




We are a thought.

An aspiration originally dreamt by our forefathers at Rotary International. An aspiration backed by our parent club, Rotary Club of Nashik, Ambad. An aspiration to open the doors for professional development and networking within and beyond the community.




We are a medium.

A medium to manifest significant ideas into actionable campaigns. Campaigns such as sustainability drives and impactful initiatives that today, would be small steps towards a better and brighter tomorrow.




We are an idea.

An invigorating idea to harness the Power of Youth to tackle problems within the society and brainstorm solutions to bring about local albeit profound changes.



We are the Agents of Change.   
We are the Force of Youth.
We are Nashik Youth Force.
